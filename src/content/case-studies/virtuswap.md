# VirtuSwap: DeFi Exchange Visual Identity

As **Brand & UI/UX Designer (Contractor)** at VirtuSwap in 2022, I designed the complete visual system for a DeFi protocol built on proprietary RPVP technology.

---

## 🎯 Challenge

Most DeFi projects land in one of two categories: technically impressive but visually forgettable, or visually striking but lacking substance. VirtuSwap — founded by finance professors, backed by top-tier investors — needed both.

The brand had to communicate technical credibility while being a product people actually wanted to use.

---

## 🛠️ Process

### Brand Identity
Developed logo (3 prototypes → final), brand book (color system, typography, logo guidelines), and a cohesive visual language rooted in the team's core values: **intelligence, efficiency, elegance**.

Color: orange (energy, ambition). Theme: Roman (precision, authority). Together: momentum and gravitas.

### Visual Assets
Produced Medium article thumbnails (2–3/week at launch), Twitter covers, airdrop graphics, token logo, and merchandise (t-shirts, sweatshirts, polo shirts, hats).

### Landing Page
One-page scroll design with dark/orange palette, responsive across desktop and mobile. Translated complex DeFi mechanics into a narrative that worked for both technical and non-technical audiences.

---

## 📦 Deliverables

- Logo system + brand book
- Weekly content graphics (Medium, Twitter)
- Token logo · Airdrop graphics · Merchandise
- Landing page (desktop + mobile)

---

## 📈 Outcome

A complete visual system built from the ground up — one brand language across every surface, from protocol documentation to physical merchandise.
