# Initia Ecosystem: Scaling Brand & Visual UX from Zero to Mainnet

As **Head of Design** at Initia (23Labs) from August 2023 to January 2026, I defined the brand identity and product suite for a next-generation L1 blockchain ecosystem.

---

## 🎯 Challenges & Objectives
Ecosystems on L1 blockchains are notoriously complex. Users typically struggle with:
1. Fragmented wallet creation and bridging processes.
2. Visual inconsistency between third-party DEXs, block explorers, and network dashboards.
3. High friction onboarding flows that scare off retail users.

Our objective was to design a **unified, cohesive ecosystem** where bridging, swapping, and portfolio tracking felt like a single, seamless application.

---

We established a comprehensive visual design system from the ground up:

---

## 📦 Products Designed & Shipped
I led end-to-end product design from initial wireframe layouts to production-ready front-end spec handoff for:

### 1. Initia Wallet
*   Simplified the onboarding flow to reduce drop-offs.
*   Designed low-friction key creation and social-login recovery structures.

### 2. InitiaDEX & Cross-Chain Bridge
*   Combined transaction status, gas estimations, and slippage controls into single-view widgets.
*   Allowed users to move and swap assets across rollup chains with clear transaction progress animations.

### 3. Portfolio Dashboard & Analytics
*   Built a command-center interface allowing operators and retail investors to monitor token balances, liquidity positions, and transaction histories in one dashboard.

## 📈 Key Results
- **Testnet Volume:** Successfully attracted over **190,000+ testnet wallets** during the public testing phase.
- **Visual Consistency:** Maintained a 100% unified visual identity across product dashboards, brand marketing materials, and ecosystem landing pages.
