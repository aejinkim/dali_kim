# Fount Robo-Advisor: FinTech Platform Scaling to 1.5T KRW AUM

As **Lead Product Designer** (Head of Design) at Fount Inc. from August 2020 to March 2021, I led product design and branding for South Korea’s leading AI-driven wealth management and Robo-Advisor platform.

---

## 🎯 Context & Challenge
Fount manages complex asset portfolios using algorithmic and AI asset allocations. The core design challenges were:
1. **Low Financial Literacy:** Wealth management felt distant and difficult for everyday retail users.
2. **Trust & Transparency:** Displaying algorithmic allocations and market fluctuations without causing panic.
3. **Conversion Drop-offs:** Complex registration and KYC (Know Your Customer) steps led to onboarding drop-offs.

---

## 🛠️ My Role & Hybrid PM Responsibilities
In a lean, fast-paced team, I wore multiple hats:
- **Product Design & Branding:** Oversaw all visual languages across the mobile application (iOS/Android) and web surfaces.
- **Product Management:** Acted as a hybrid PM, handling product planning, requirements drafting, QA tracking, and A/B testing coordination.

---

## 🔧 Solution & Process

> [!NOTE]
> **Onboarding Simplification:**
> We overhauled the registration and account opening funnel. By transforming static forms into a conversational interface with micro-animations, we reduced friction points significantly.

- **Portfolio Visualization:** Designed an interactive asset allocation wheel that visually grouped asset exposure (e.g. global stocks, gold, bonds) into intuitive, color-coded categories.
- **A/B Testing:** Iterated on investment recommendation cards to find the optimal phrasing that increased the rate of active deposit completion.

---

## ✅ Results & Impact
- **Asset Growth:** Supported Fount's growth to achieve **1.5 Trillion KRW (≈$1.1B USD)** in assets under management (AUM).
- **Market Leadership:** Consolidated Fount's position as the leading Robo-Advisor platform in AUM within South Korea.
