# Satrec Initiative: Satellite Ground Control Segment UX

As a **Product Designer** at Satrec Initiative from January 2013 to January 2018, I led end-to-end design for Satellite Ground Control Systems across **10+ domestic and international space missions**.

---

## 🎯 Context: Designing for High Stakes
Ground segment control software is extremely complex. Operators monitor dozens of telemetry parameters (altitude, thermal state, battery health, solar panel angles) and issue flight commands.
- **The Challenge:** Operator cognitive load is extremely high. Bad design doesn't just mean a poor user experience — it can lead to mission failure or loss of satellite contact.
- **The Goal:** Turn massive, real-time telemetry streams into clean, visual, and actionable flight dashboards.

---

## 🛠️ Products Designed & Shipped

### 1. GEO-KOMPSAT (Chollian) Ground Segment Control System
I served as the Lead Product Designer for the main mission control segment interfaces, organizing multi-monitor operator terminals to support dual status monitoring and orbital forecasting.

> [!WARNING]
> **Cognitive Load Reduction:**
> Operators need to spot system anomalies in milliseconds. We introduced color-coded criticality levels (Normal, Alert, Critical) and customizable widget grids, replacing endless rows of raw terminal logs.

### 2. Integrated Space Weather Forecasting System
Designed and implemented an operational dashboard for space weather forecasting, showing radiation levels, geomagnetic storm warnings, and solar wind velocities.

### 3. Satellite Simulation & Orbital Analysis Products
Designed 3D orbital trajectory simulation interfaces, helping telemetry teams preview satellite passes and plan ground station downlinks.

---

## 📈 Impact & Core Takeaways
- **10+ Space Missions:** Shipped mission-critical software actively used in production ground control centers worldwide.
- **Complexity to Clarity:** Proved that the same core design principles of hierarchy, visual pacing, and direct manipulation can be applied to aerospace telemetry screens as to commercial applications.
