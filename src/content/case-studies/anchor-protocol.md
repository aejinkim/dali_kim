# Anchor Protocol: Redesigning DeFi Savings to $16B+ TVL

During my tenure as **Senior Product Designer** at Terraform Labs (March 2021 – January 2023), I owned product design for **Anchor Protocol**, which grew to become Terra's flagship DeFi savings product.

---

## 🎯 The Core UX Problem
At the time, decentralized finance (DeFi) was complex, jargon-heavy, and intimidating to average users. To interact with DeFi protocols, users had to understand:
- Liquidation ratios and collateralized debt positions (CDPs).
- Variable interest rate dynamics.
- Advanced wallet interactions and gas optimizations.

Our goal was to design a product billed as the **"Stripe for Savings"** — bringing stable, high-yield yield to mainstream depositors.

---

## 🛠️ The Design Solution
We redesigned the core UX to resemble a modern web banking application:

> [!TIP]
> **Minimalist Dashboard:**
> By replacing complex graphs with a clean, single-number balance card and two clear action buttons—**"Deposit"** and **"Withdraw"**—we demystified the mechanics of yield generation.

```
+-------------------------------------------------+
|  Anchor Protocol Savings                        |
|                                                 |
|  Total Balance:  $45,219.00  USD                |
|  APY:            ~19.5%                         |
|                                                 |
|  [ Deposit ]                      [ Withdraw ]  |
+-------------------------------------------------+
```

---

## 🏗️ Technical & Brand Deliverables
- **Cross-Chain Bridge UX:** Redesigned the Terra Bridge transaction panels, turning cross-chain transfers into simple step-by-step wizards with progress animations.
- **Investor & Partner Collateral:** Spearheaded visual assets and high-stakes presentations for investment partners and ecosystem builders.
- **Anchor Wallet:** Designed simplified wallet interactions, enabling seamless browser extension confirmations.

---

## 📈 Impact & Scale
- **Total Value Locked (TVL):** Reached a peak of over **$16 Billion USD** in deposits.
- **User Adoption:** Lowered onboarding friction to allow mainstream, non-crypto-native savers to successfully deposit funds.
